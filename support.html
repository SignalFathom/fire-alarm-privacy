How to use

  1. Start alarm: Pull the on-screen handle down until it clicks.
  2. Open settings: Tap the keyhole lock to rotate and open the sound menu.
  3. Select a sound: Tap any alarm/voice option. The menu closes and the lock returns.
  4. Play selected sound: Pull the handle again to start the newly selected looping alarm.
  5. Stop alarm: Tap anywhere outside the alarm box while it’s sounding.
  6. Volume: Use your device volume buttons. (App has no in-app volume.)

Notes

  • All audio is bundled and works offline. No internet is required.
  • Small clicks play when the lock rotates and when the handle completes a pull.
  • If you add new audio files, fully restart the app to load them.

FAQ

No sound?
Check your device volume and silent/vibrate switch. Some Android devices need Media volume turned up.

New alarm not playing after I select it?
Open settings via the lock, tap your sound, then pull the handle again. If it still doesn’t change, force-close and reopen the app.

Does the app collect data?
No. See the privacy policy below.

Can I use this during drills?
Yes—this is intended for demonstrations and training. Obey local safety policies.
